Collection Verification

Personal contact

In order to verify your collection, we may need to get in touch with you. In at least one of the fields below, please enter your personal username so that we may be able to contact you.

External minting

If you have minted the NFTs from your collection externally (i.e. not on Aiko.Art), paste the Candy machine ID(s) and/or mint hashes below. Alternatively, if you have minted your entire collectio on Aiko.Art, there is no need to fill the fields below, you can skip this step. In the event that you minted one part of your collection on Aiko.Art and another part externally, paste, the Candy machine ID(s) and/or mint hashes of the externally minted NFTs only.


Proof of authorship

To verify your collection, we requiere definite proof that you are the author of its artworks. This includes files that clearly show that you made the artwork within this collection. Please collect all files that are relevant into one folder, put it on a cloud storage service (preferably Google Drive) and paste the access link below. Make sure the link is open and has no expiration date.
